Good to know

Common questions

Is the first session really free?

Yes. One session, on us. No card, nothing to sign, and no sales pitch when you arrive.

I have never done Pilates. Can I still come?

Absolutely. A lot of our members started as complete beginners, or came back to fitness after a long break. For your first few visits, come during staffed hours and a coach will be on the floor to get you set up.

How does it actually work?

We are open 24 hours and the sessions are self-guided. You choose from 800+ instructor-led workouts on the screen at your reformer, from 5-minute quick ones to 50-minute full-body flows. Book in the app, fob the door, and the room is yours.

What if I want a hand while I am there?

We have staffed hours Monday to Friday, 9 to 11am and 4 to 7pm, and on weekends 7 to 10am. Book your free session in one of those windows and there will be a real person on the floor.

Am I locked into anything?

Not at all. If you decide to join after your free session, memberships are week to week, with no lock-in and no cancellation fees.
